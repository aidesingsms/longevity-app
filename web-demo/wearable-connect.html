About Wearable Integration

This application uses platform-specific APIs to connect with your wearable devices:

Apple HealthKit: Requires iOS 16+ and HealthKit permissions. Data is synced via HKHealthStore API.

Samsung Health: Uses Samsung Health SDK for Galaxy Watch and Samsung Health app integration via HealthDataStore.

Other Devices: Fitbit, Garmin, Oura, and Whoop use their respective REST APIs with OAuth 2.0 authentication.
